# Bank Customer Support Knowledge Base

## Account Types

- **Personal Checking Account**: No monthly fee with direct deposit, $5 otherwise. Includes debit card and online banking.
- **Savings Account**: 0.5% APY interest rate, $100 minimum balance to open, $3 monthly fee if balance falls below $100.
- **Business Account**: $15 monthly fee, includes 200 free transactions per month, $0.25 per transaction after.

## Transaction Inquiries

Customers can view recent transactions through:

1. Mobile app (real-time updates)
2. Online banking portal (updated daily)
3. Monthly statements (sent via email or mail)
4. Branch visits (ask for transaction history printout)

Recent transactions show: date, merchant name, amount, and transaction type (purchase, withdrawal, deposit, transfer).

## Account Access Issues

If locked out of account:

1. Use "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Call customer service at 1-800-555-0123
3. Visit any branch with government ID
4. Account will be locked after 3 failed attempts for 30 minutes

## Card Services

- **Lost/Stolen Cards**: Report immediately at 1-800-555-0199, 24/7
- **Card Replacement**: $10 fee, arrives in 5-7 business days
- **Fraud Alerts**: Text alerts for suspicious transactions, reply "YES" to confirm or "NO" to report fraud

## Loan Inquiries

- **Personal Loans**: Up to $50,000, 6.99% - 15.99% APR, terms 12-60 months
- **Auto Loans**: Starting at 4.99% APR for qualified buyers, up to 72 months
- **Mortgage**: 30-year fixed at 6.5% APR, 15-year fixed at 5.75% APR
- Approval typically takes 2-3 business days for personal loans, 30-45 days for mortgages

## Billing Issues

Common billing issues and solutions:

1. **Unauthorized charges**: File dispute within 60 days, temporary credit within 10 business days
2. **Overdraft fees**: $35 per item, max 4 per day. Can be waived for first offense with good account standing
3. **Payment processing**: Electronic payments post within 1-2 business days, checks take 3-5 days

## Account Statement

Statements include:

- Account summary (beginning/ending balance)
- Transaction details
- Fees charged
- Interest earned (savings accounts)
- Available 24/7 through online banking
- Paper statements cost $2 per month, can be waived with e-statement enrollment
